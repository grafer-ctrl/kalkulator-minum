Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the water calculator?

Our calculator uses scientifically-based formulas that consider your weight, age, and activity level. However, individual needs may vary based on health conditions, climate, and personal factors.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes! We don't store any of your personal information. All calculations happen in your browser, and we don't collect or transmit your data to our servers.

Can I use this for medical advice?

Our tool is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Consult your healthcare provider for specific health concerns.

How do I get notifications?

Enable browser notifications when prompted, and our system will send you hourly reminders during your waking hours (6 AM to 10 PM).
